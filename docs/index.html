Privacy Policy — LoanSolver

Effective date: 13 July 2026

LoanSolver ("the app") is an EMI / loan calculator published by Geo Paulson (Ghonestapps). This policy explains, in plain language, how the app handles your information.

The short version

LoanSolver collects no data. None. The app has no internet permission, no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party SDKs.

What data the app collects

Nothing. The loan amounts, interest rates, and periods you type are processed entirely on your device and are never transmitted anywhere. The app does not collect, store remotely, share, or sell any personal or non-personal data.

PDF reports

When you tap Share, the app generates a PDF summary of your calculation on your device. That file leaves your phone only if you choose to share it, through an app you pick (e.g. WhatsApp, Gmail). From that point the receiving app's privacy policy applies. LoanSolver itself never sees or uploads the file.

Permissions

The app requests no sensitive permissions — no location, no contacts, no storage access beyond its own private cache, no camera, no microphone.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children. Its content is a general-purpose financial calculator.

Disclaimer

Calculations are provided for reference only and may differ from a lender's actual figures. LoanSolver does not offer loans, credit, or any financial product, and does not provide financial advice.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled, this page will be updated before that version is released.

Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue at github.com/Geopaul94/EMICalculator.
